🎤 VOICE COMMANDS (Chrome/Edge only):

Say these during workouts:
• "Start recording" - Begin camera
• "Stop recording" - End recording  
• "Log set" - Quick manual log
• "Next exercise" - Skip to next
• "Show history" - View progress
• "Pause" / "Resume" - Control workout

Enable in: Settings → Voice Commands

💡 TIPS:

First Load:

• Green "✅ Camera & Voice Ready!" appears when loaded

Camera Position:
• Portrait mode (vertical)
• Full body visible
• 6-10 feet from phone
• Good lighting
• Stable surface

Battery:
• Camera uses ~30-45% per hour
• Disable when not needed

Storage:
• Videos: ~100-200 MB per set
• Configurable retention (Settings)
• Can delete old videos anytime

🔧 TROUBLESHOOTING:

❌ Blank page?
→ Add icons! (see STEP 1)

❌ "Camera system loading..."?
→ Wait full 60 seconds on first load
→ Refresh page if still waiting

❌ Camera won't start?
→ Must be HTTPS (Netlify provides)
→ Check browser camera permissions
→ Try different browser (Chrome works best)

❌ Voice not working?
→ Chrome/Edge only (not Safari)
→ Check microphone permissions
→ Background noise can interfere

❌ Form analysis shows wrong grade?
→ Ensure full body visible
→ Good lighting required
→ Camera stable (not hand-held)
